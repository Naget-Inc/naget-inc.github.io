Transforming Search & Discovery

A New Approach to Relevance

Users face information overload online and struggle with complex queries that yield generic results. The dominance of SEO-optimized content prioritizes algorithm manipulation over user value, while the industry's focus on 'chatty search bars' has created barriers to meaningful search innovations in the generative AI era. Traditional search signals based on clicks and user engagement metrics are not the moat they appear to be. We believe the future of search lies in understanding content at a deeper level with much more compute per query.
